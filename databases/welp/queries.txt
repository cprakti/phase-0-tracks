
Query:

SELECT businesses.name,
       users.first_name,
       users.last_name,
       reviews.stars,
       reviews.comment
FROM reviews
INNER JOIN users
    on users.id = reviews.user_id
INNER JOIN businesses
    on businesses.id = reviews.business_id;

Query:
SELECT businesses.name,
         users.first_name,
         users.last_name,
         reviews.stars,
         reviews.comment
  FROM reviews
  INNER JOIN users
      on users.id = reviews.user_id
  INNER JOIN businesses
      on businesses.id = reviews.business_id
  WHERE businesses.id = 1;


Output:
Grundy Hollow Wedding Chapel|Mandy|Kaling|5|Great service!
